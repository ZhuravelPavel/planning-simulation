## Gates & Decision Points

### Gate 1: Week 0 Domain Purchase
**Question:** Did we secure the domain?
- ✅ YES → Proceed
- ❌ NO → Select backup domain, purchase immediately

### Gate 2: Week 15 Technical SEO
**Question:** Is site technically ready?
**Checklist:**
- [ ] PageSpeed score >85
- [ ] Mobile-friendly
- [ ] All pages indexed
- [ ] Analytics tracking

**If YES:** ✅ Proceed to launch
**If NO:** ⚠️ Fix issues (shouldn't take more than 1 day)

### Gate 3: Month 6 Critical Evaluation
**Question:** Is SEO showing promise?

**Metrics to Check:**
- [ ] 50+ organic visitors/month?
- [ ] 10+ organic signups?
- [ ] Any content gaining traction?
- [ ] Existing user conversion healthy?
- [ ] Time investment sustainable?

**Decision Matrix:**
| Traffic | Conversions | Existing Business | **Decision** |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|--------------|
| >50/mo | >10 | >90% target | **Path A: SCALE** |
| 20-50 | <10 | 80-100% target | **Path B: SUSTAIN** |
| <20 | <5 | <80% target | **Path C: PAUSE** |

### Gate 4: Month 12 Performance Review
**Question:** Did scaling work? Should we continue?

**Metrics to Check:**
- [ ] 500+ organic visitors/month?
- [ ] 50+ organic signups (M7-12)?
- [ ] Time investment sustainable?
- [ ] ROI trending positive?

**If YES:** ✅ Continue to Phase 4 (Maturity)
**If MIXED:** ⚠️ Adjust strategy, consider sustaining mode
**If NO:** ❌ Pause or pivot to different growth channel

### Gate 5: Month 24 Final Milestone
**Question:** Is SEO a proven, sustainable channel?

**Success Criteria:**
- [ ] 2,000+ organic visitors/month
- [ ] $2,400+ MRR from organic
- [ ] 25+ keywords in top 20
- [ ] 40+ referring domains
- [ ] Process is sustainable (not burning out)

**If YES:** ✅ SEO matured, continue investing
**If NO:** Evaluate what went wrong, adjust for Year 3

## Risk Timeline: When Things Could Go Wrong

| Month | Potential Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|-------|----------------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **Week 0-2** | Domain unavailable | 5% | Minor delay | Have 3 backup options |
| **Week 13-15** | Pages take longer to create | 10% | 3-5 day delay | Use AI assistance heavily |
| **Month 1-3** | No organic traffic at all | 20% | Discouraging but expected | Be patient, keep publishing |
| **Month 6** | Results insufficient to scale | 30-40% | Strategy adjustment | Have sustain/pause plan ready |
| **Month 7-12** | Can't sustain time investment | 25% | Need to hire or slow down | Budget for writer, or reduce cadence |
| **Month 12** | Writer quality issues | 15% (if hired) | Wasted budget | Vet carefully, start with trial articles |
| **Month 13-24** | Google algorithm change | 10% | Temporary ranking drop | Focus on quality, adapt strategy |
| **Any time** | Burnout from overcommitment | 20-30% | Pause needed | Be honest about capacity, adjust early |
